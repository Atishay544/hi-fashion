## Strategic Calls (Hi Fashion specific)

### 1. Don't fight marketplaces on head terms
"kurti online" → Myntra page 1. Unwinnable in <1 year. Win **long-tail + intent + brand + Hinglish** instead.

### 2. Category pages first, product pages second
Category/collection pages rank for high-volume, high-intent queries and funnel to product pages. Start here.

### 3. Merchant Center is the #1 early move for a live store
Google Shopping free listings can drive purchase-intent traffic before organic rankings build. A D2C store with a clean feed and Product schema gets double coverage (Shopping tab + organic snippet).

### 4. COD as a positioning + query wedge
"[item] cash on delivery online india" — real search intent in India with lower competition than generic "[item] online india". Surface COD explicitly in:
- Category page copy ("available with Cash on Delivery")
- Product schema `paymentAccepted`
- Merchant Center attribute
- A dedicated trust section

### 5. Reviews unlock AggregateRating — but only when real
Plan a Resend post-delivery email asking for review. Once 10+ real reviews exist, add AggregateRating schema for star ratings in SERPs. Never fake it — Google penalizes.

### 6. Hinglish content tier
3–5 Hinglish blog posts (e.g. "Saste Trendy Kapde Online India 2026") — near-zero D2C competition, real search volume in Tier 2/3.

### 7. Seasonality planning
Start creating festive/seasonal category pages 6–8 weeks before peak seasons:
- Diwali festive collection → September
- Wedding season → October
- Summer collection → March
- Valentine's Day → December

## Risk Register

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Algorithm updates | Diversify across brand + category + long-tail, not single pages |
| Marketplace SERP dominance | Win long-tail + COD + occasion + Hinglish where they're thin |
| Thin product descriptions | Unique copy template per product category; prioritize top sellers |
| Out-of-stock products | Keep indexed (availability=OutOfStock in schema); don't 404 |
| No reviews yet | Cannot use AggregateRating; plan post-delivery email collection |
| Founder bandwidth | Automate feed; templated category copy; batch guide writing |
| Fake-discount risk | ASCI + Consumer Protection rules — no inflated MRPs, no fake reviews |
